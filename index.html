Free & open source

You are not alone.

You Are Loved uses on-device AI to watch over your screen, close what shouldn't be open, and reach your accountability partner — instantly. Everything runs locally. Your data never leaves your machine.

Install on Mac  →

Free. One command. Takes 2 minutes.

AI that watches with you

Seven layers of on-device intelligence — visual recognition, real-time OCR, and contextual AI classification — scan your screen every five seconds. All processing happens locally on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is stored remotely.

Reaches your partner

The moment something is detected, your accountability partner receives an alert — via Telegram or email, instantly. They see a blurred notification, never raw content. You chose them because you trust them.

Stays on

Built to be impossible to remove without your partner's approval. It survives restarts, terminal closures, and force-quits. A companion watchdog restores it if tampered with. Because you asked it to be this way.

Privacy

Everything runs on your device.

Detection is powered entirely by local AI models running on your Mac — NudeNet for visual recognition, Tesseract for text extraction, and Claude for contextual understanding. No screenshots are sent to any server. Alert images sent to your partner are heavily blurred before they leave your machine. Your partner's details, your configuration, your history — all of it lives only on your device. We have no servers. We collect no data.

Install

One command. That's it.

bash <(curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Building-Humanity-Up/youareloved/main/install.sh)

macOS 12 or later. Apple Silicon and Intel supported. You'll be asked for your accountability partner's contact details. The uninstall code goes to them directly — not to you.
